# Helm UI Component Specification

## Buttons

### Primary Button
Background: Helm Blue 700
Text: White
Corner radius: 14px
Hover: Helm Blue 500
Active: Helm Blue 900

Usage:
- Primary CTAs
- Critical actions

---

### Secondary Button
Border: Helm Blue 500
Text: Helm Blue 700
Background: Transparent

Hover: Soft blue tint background (5% opacity)

---

### Tertiary Button
Text only
Muted color
Used for inline actions

---

### Gold Accent Button (Pro)
Background: Gold 500
Text: Dark Slate
Used only for:
- Upgrade prompts
- Pro features

---

## Cards

Background:
Light mode: White
Dark mode: Panel color

Radius: 16px
Shadow: Soft, subtle

Card variants:
- Standard
- Highlighted (Blue border)
- Pro (Gold corner badge)

---

## Lists

List rows:
Padding: 14px vertical
Divider: 1px neutral divider

Hover state:
Soft blue tint background (3%)

Selected:
Left indicator bar in Helm Blue

---

## Alerts

### Info
Left border: Blue
Background: 5% blue tint

### Warning
Left border: Gold
Background: 5% gold tint

### Critical
Left border: Red (#D64545)
Background: 5% red tint
